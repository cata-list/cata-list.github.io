Arthur C. Clarke

Arthur C. Clarke was a British science fiction writer, science writer and futurist, inventor, undersea explorer, and television series host. He is famous for being co-writer of the screenplay for the 1968 film '2001: A Space Odyssey', widely considered to be one of the most influential films of all time.
