Joseph Campbell

Joseph Campbell was an American mythologist, writer, and lecturer, best known for his work in comparative mythology and comparative religion. His work covers many aspects of the human experience. Campbell's magnum opus is his book 'The Hero with a Thousand Faces' (1949), in which he discusses his theory of the journey of the archetypal hero found in world mythologies.
