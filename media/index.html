A British television documentary series which focuses on the work of psychoanalysts Sigmund Freud and Anna Freud, and PR consultant Edward Bernays. Adam Curtis, writer and director of the series, explains the series as being about 'how those in power have used Freud's theories to try and control the dangerous crowd in an age of mass democracy'.
